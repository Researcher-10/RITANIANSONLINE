What are School Announcements?

Good Day! Here, people who visit this website and click on "Announcements" button will be informed on what is currently happening inside Sta. Rita National High School.


Announcements are made so that people involved outside and inside the school will be notified of an occurence inside the school that may concern them. It is important that announcements are always up to date.


Please scroll down to view the school announcements and events, thank you!
